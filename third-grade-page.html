3rd Grade Adventures

These thinkers are discussing the Plebeians and the Patricians of the Ancient Roman Republic. We have finished our constitution flag and can understand some of those big words and ideas that are in the preamble of the Constitution. We have stared 'reading drawers,' which will give us some extra practice with spelling vowel digraphs. Our comprehension skills are growing as we identify the main idea and key details in stories about communities around the world.
