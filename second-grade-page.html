2nd Grade Adventures

Super Seconds are finishing their tabla drums, from ancient India. This has been a fun project, although a bit messy. We are making connections between historical events as we read passages about the origin of our flag and the Mayflower. These Super Second graders are crafting floating candle dishes, in celebration of the ancient India festival of lights, Diwali.
