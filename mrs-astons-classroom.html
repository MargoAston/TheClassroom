Welcome

Welcome to your classroom portal and the 2022/23 school year. My name is Mrs. Aston and I am super excited that we will be learning and exploring together. This website will help us stay connected when you are at home or on the go. You will find:

  • videos and activities
  • Montessori math
  • silly stories
  • exciting adventures
  • suspenseful mysteries
  • step-by-step writing opportunities
  • helpful spelling games

Supplies You May Need:

These are a few things that you may need as you work on your activities this week:

  • Pencil
  • Eraser
  • Journal or Lined Paper
  • Crayons
  • Scissors
  • Glue Stick

Let's Get Started

You are ready to begin. Check out the tabs at the top of the page. Click on your grade. You will find an online 'work-plan.' Look around. Take a minute to see what we are exploring this week. Choose an activity to start your day. Take a break from time to time. Most of all... Have FUN!

A Note to Parents:

This classroom portal is designed to give you a glimpse into the daily classroom activities and to make connections with at home learning. Activities are based on the multiple learning modalities of your little scholar. You are the primary educator. All activities and lessons are suggestions. You are welcome to change, add, or twist any and all assignments. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to ask. It is a privilege to be your partner in education.
